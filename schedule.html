The Structure of the Boot Camp

The I Python Boot Camp will run in two days and will be organized in two parts:

  • I: Lecture Sessions: Lectures providing pedagogical introductions to the problems that will be tackled during the hands-on sessions.
  • II: Hands-on Sessions: The participants will develop examples and apply them to real problems.
